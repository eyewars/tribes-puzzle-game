Unusual Noise

Realization hits you. "It’s their language," you say.

The tribespeople smile, nodding in approval. "Yes. Our neighbors speak in clicks, just as we mark our stories on skin."

They motion for you to come closer and unroll a rough map on the ground. With careful gestures, they show you the right path—your tribe is closer than you thought.

Grateful, you thank them for their kindness and prepare to leave. "Safe travels," they say as you part ways.

At last, after all this time, you are heading home.
