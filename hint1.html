A Revelation

You step closer to the totems, tracing the carvings with your fingers. Each one tells a story—legends of ancestors, battles fought, spirits honored. But one stands out. The patterns are unfamiliar, and yet, something about them feels connected to the riddle.

And then it clicks. No home. Carries everything on his back. The answer was in front of you all along.

You turn away, ready to retrace your steps, to find the man again. But as you near the village exit, he is already there—waiting. A knowing smirk rests on his face.

He speaks, his voice steady, expectant.

"I have no home, yet I carry all I own on my back. What am I?"

This time, you know.
