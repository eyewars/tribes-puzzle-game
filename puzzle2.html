A Guide Through the Unknown

You follow the path toward the unknown tribe, but as you glance back, you notice something odd—your so-called friend isn’t coming with you. He simply stands there, watching, an unreadable expression on his face.

"You go on ahead," he says with a smirk. "This part isn’t for me."

Before you can question him, he turns and disappears down another road.

You push forward, and soon, the distant sound of rhythmic drumming reaches your ears. As you step into the clearing, the sight before you is mesmerizing—people dancing in a wild, hypnotic rhythm, painted figures moving around a towering fire. Their bodies are covered in intricate markings, swirls of ink etched into their skin, each telling a story you cannot yet understand.

The tribe notices you immediately, but instead of hostility, they greet you warmly. A woman with spiraling symbols across her arms steps forward. "You came at a special time," she says. "We are in the middle of our ritual. Would you like to join us?"
