A Guide Through the Unknown

As you step into your village, familiar faces rush toward you. Gasps of relief, joyful laughter, warm embraces.

"Omg, we missed you so much!" someone exclaims. "Where were you?"

You smile, feeling the weight of your journey settle. "I woke up in the middle of nowhere," you explain, "but on my way back, I learned so much about other tribes—their ways, their stories, their people."

To celebrate your return, a great feast is prepared. The village fills with music and laughter, firelight flickering as the night deepens.

Then, through the crowd, a familiar figure approaches.

The traveler.

A grin on his face, he holds out a wooden cup filled with a strange brownish liquid. "Quite the journey, huh?" he says. "Here, drink. You’ve earned it."

You hesitate. Should you trust him?
